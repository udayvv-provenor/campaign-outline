SLA Ladder & Guardrails

SLA Ladder

  • ≤ 5 min
    Auto-acknowledgement · welcome email with expectations & pre-read links
  • ≤ 4 hr
    First human response · SDR outbound call or personalized email
  • ≤ 24 hr
    Calendar hold · SA + Client Partner tentative slot booked · agenda sent
  • ≤ 48 hr
    SA intro call · scoping questions, confirm pre-read · qualification notes complete
  • ≤ 5 BD
    Workshop delivered (Tier-1) · 90-min diagnostic · scorecard + roadmap handed over
  • ≤ 10 BD
    Proposal drafted · Helio assure implementation proposal + 90-day sprint plan

Guardrails

Capacity cap. ~15 ABM-grade workshops/month. Overflow routed to senior-SA pool or Wave 2.
Opportunity-first. Every fast-path intake creates an opportunity record before workshop is booked.
Weekly triage review. Client Partner + MOps review inbound pipeline every Friday.
72-hour escalation. No SA contact within 72 hr? Auto-escalation to Client Partner.
Reporting re-entry. Fast-path conversions report at S3 (webinar) or S6 (workshop) so funnel math stays unified, but lane source is tagged.
Content inheritance. Fast-Path B skips S1–S5 content but SA pulls matching industry CS + brochure for pre-read.
