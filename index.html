Guardrails

Capacity cap — Fast-Path B capped at 8 active workshops/month per SA-CP pair to preserve quality.
Opportunity-first — Opportunity record created at triage, not after the workshop.
Weekly triage review — RevOps + Marketing review dispositions weekly to prevent leakage.
Escalation path — Any hand-raise from a named Tier-1 account → same-day SA review.
Reporting re-entry — Fast-path outcomes re-join the main funnel at S3 or S6 with source tagged.
Content inheritance — Fast-path invitees still receive Field Guide + workshop prep kit.
